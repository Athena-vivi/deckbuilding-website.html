Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I report a bug?
A: Use the contact form above with "Bug Report" category. Include your device, browser, and detailed steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots are also helpful!
Q: Can I suggest new ships or features?
A: Absolutely! We love community suggestions. Use the "Feature Suggestion" category and describe your idea in detail. While we can't implement every suggestion, we carefully consider all feedback.
Q: Is the game really free?
A: Yes! Deckbuilding is completely free to play with no in-app purchases or premium content. We're supported by advertising revenue, which helps us keep the game free for everyone.
Q: Will you add multiplayer support?
A: Multiplayer is definitely something we're considering for future updates! It's a complex feature that requires significant development time, but it's on our roadmap.
Q: How often do you update the game?
A: We aim to release updates every few weeks with bug fixes, balance changes, and new content. Major feature updates may take longer but are worth the wait!
Q: Can I play offline?
A: Currently, an internet connection is required to play. However, the game uses minimal data and works well on slower connections.
